Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before starting

Our standard setup takes 5-7 business days after receiving all required information. The complete process from consultation to first sale typically takes 3-4 weeks.

All our methods and products are 100% halal compliant. While we cannot guarantee specific earnings (as that depends on market conditions), we guarantee our full support until you make your first sale.

No experience is needed! Our mentorship program is designed for complete beginners. We walk you through every step until you're confident managing your store.

We accept 50% upfront and 50% upon completion of your store setup. Payment methods include bank transfer, JazzCash, and EasyPaisa.
